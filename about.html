About Us

Our Story

Shrisahil Electricals & Power Solutions is a Central Government approved solar contractor & rooftop vendor. We have years of experience working on HT/LT lines, transformer installation, street lights, underground cabling and commercial & residential solar projects. Our priority: safety, quality & on-time delivery.

Mission

To accelerate solar adoption with reliable installations and transparent service.

Vision

To make clean energy accessible and dependable for homes, businesses and industries.
